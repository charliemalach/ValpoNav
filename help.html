Help

How do I navigate the map?

To navigate the map, you can click and drag to move and zoom in and out with the scroll wheel, or by using the zoom in/out buttons located in the top right corner. You can also select markers by clicking on them, searching by building name, and using the checkboxes on the menu.

How do I search for a building?

To search for a building, locate the search bar and click on "Search buildings....". After clicking the search field, enter the building name and as you enter characters, results will popup. Select the correct building and then press the search button.

How do I use the map features?

Located on the top right of the map is a toolbar. In this toolbar there are 4 options. From top to bottom there is zoom in, zoom out, geolocation, and home, which returns the view to the original view.

How do I navigate the menu?

To navigate the menu, click the hamburger menu icon in the top left. Once selected a menu will appear. Within this menu, there are categories and buildings to choose from that you can customize and seperate to distinguish buildings and locations from each other.

How can I select multiple buildings?

To select multiple buildings, open the menu in the top left and navigate the categories to check or uncheck buildings. Once you have selected the desired buildings, the markers will show on the map.

How can I find building layouts?

To find building layouts, select a marker by clicking one on the map, or clicking the name next to a checkbox and click "More Info", here is where the builing layouts are displayed
